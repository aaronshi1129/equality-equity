Equality vs Equity Game

How to Play

  1. Read each scenario carefully
  2. Decide if the scenario demonstrates equality or equity
  3. Click the corresponding button to answer
  4. Learn from the feedback and continue to the next scenario
  5. Answer within the time limit to score points
  6. Build streaks and earn badges as you play!

Understanding the Concepts

Equality

Equality means treating everyone the same way regardless of their individual circumstances, needs, or abilities.

Example: Everyone gets the same size shoes regardless of their foot size.

Equity

Equity means providing resources and opportunities based on individual needs to create fair outcomes for everyone.

Example: Everyone gets shoes that fit their specific foot size.
